Designik is a small shirt designing business located in Tabaco City, Albay.

Started in 2016, it is a small business for locals, providing high quality materials and original designs.

We believe through this Designik we can help to elevate individual style.

Mission:

To create uniquely crafted and high-quality shirts
that inspire self-expression and confidence in our
customers, fostering a sense of individuality and style.

Vision:

To be recognized as a go-to destination for innovative and personalized shirt designs, known for our commitment to creativity, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Goal:

Achieve a 20% growth in customer base within the next year through a combination of online marketing strategies, customer engagement initiatives, and expanding our product offerings to cater to diverse tastes.
